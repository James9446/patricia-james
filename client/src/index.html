Venue History

The Presidio Officers' Club is a historic Spanish Colonial Revival building that served as the social center for military officers and their families. Built in the 1930s, it features beautiful adobe walls, dark wood beams, and wrought iron fixtures that create an elegant and historic atmosphere.

The venue offers both indoor and outdoor spaces, with stunning views of the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco Bay.

Directions & Parking

From Downtown SF: Take Lombard Street west to the Presidio, follow signs to Officers' Club.

From Golden Gate Bridge: Take the first exit after the bridge, follow Lincoln Boulevard to Moraga Avenue.

Parking: Complimentary parking available in the Presidio. Valet service available for the event.

Public Transit: Muni bus routes 28, 29, and 43 serve the Presidio.
